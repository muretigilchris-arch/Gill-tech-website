Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

What is your design process?

I start with research—understanding your users, goals, and competitors. Then I create wireframes, design mockups, and interactive prototypes. You'll give feedback at every stage before final delivery.

How long does a typical project take?

Most projects take 4–8 weeks depending on complexity. A simple landing page might take 2 weeks. A full app redesign could take 3 months. I'll give you a clear timeline after our first conversation.

Do you also do development?

I specialize in UI/UX design but collaborate closely with developers. I can recommend trusted developers or work seamlessly with your existing team. Handoff files are always developer-ready.

What do you charge?

Every project is different. Pricing depends on scope, complexity, and timeline. Book a free consultation and I'll give you a detailed quote tailored to your needs—no pressure.
